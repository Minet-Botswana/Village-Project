Insurance Covers

Complete details of our insurance coverage options

Homeowners Insurance Cover

What We Cover

  • Main residence and outbuildings at your address
  • Garages and fixed carports
  • Home offices and tool sheds
  • Domestic workers' living quarters
  • Walls, metal palisades, gates and fences
  • Fixtures and fittings, including built-in furniture
  • Security systems affixed to the home

Perils Covered

  • Fire, lightning, or explosion
  • Storm, wind, water, flood, hail
  • Impact by trees following storm or wind

Additional Benefits

  • Bereavement expenses: Max P5,000 per person, P20,000 per event
  • House contents: Max P10,000 per incident, P20,000 per year
  • Special alterations after disability: Max P20,000
  • Fire-fighting charges
  • Removal of rubble and wreckage

Not Covered

  • Theft, attempted theft, and malicious damage
  • Thatch roofs and non-standard buildings
  • Properties unoccupied for 30+ consecutive days
  • Faulty design or workmanship
  • Damage from power cuts or load shedding
  • Pre-existing damages
Important: This policy excludes thatch roofs and covers standard construction only. Average condition applies for under-insurance.

Third Party Vehicle Insurance

Liability Coverage

  • Legal liability arising from vehicle use for:
    • Accidental death or bodily injury to any person
    • Accidental loss or damage to property
  • Costs and expenses with prior written consent
  • Liability while towing other vehicles or trailers

Coverage Limits

  • Maximum indemnity: P500,000 per event
  • Covers single events or series from same incident
  • Includes legal costs of other parties you're liable for
  • Settlement and defense costs with our consent

What's Included

  • Costs you are legally liable for
  • Legal costs of other parties
  • Defense and settlement costs (with consent)
  • Towing liability coverage

Exclusions

  • Accidents before policy start date
  • Injuries to passengers in trailers or caravans
  • Passengers outside the cab during incident
  • Injuries to insured, family, or employees
  • Damage to your own property
  • Contractual liability claims
Coverage Limit: Maximum indemnity is P500,000 per event. This applies to any single event or series of events from the same incident.
