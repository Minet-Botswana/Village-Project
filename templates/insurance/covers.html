What is Homeowners Comprehensive Insurance?
Homeowners comprehensive insurance is an extensive type of property insurance that provides coverage for your home and its contents against a wide range of risks. It offers protection not only for the physical structure of your home but also for personal belongings, liability, and additional living expenses in case your home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered event.

What is Third-Party Motor Insurance?
Third-party motor insurance is a type of car insurance policy that covers the insured's legal liability for damages caused to a third party. This is the most basic form of car insurance and is mandatory in many countries for all vehicle owners. It provides financial protection if you are held responsible for causing injury or property damage to others in an accident.
