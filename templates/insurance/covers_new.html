  • Any amount that the insured becomes legally liable to pay arising from the use of the vehicle insured in respect of:

Perils Covered

  • Fire, lightning, or explosion.
  • Storm, wind, water, flood, hail.
  • Impact by trees following storm, wind, water, floods.

Automatic Extensions

  • Bereavement expenses: Max P5000 (any one person), P20 000 (any one event) for insured events only.
  • House contents: Max P 10 000 (any one incident), up to P20, 000 (any one period) following an insured event at the insured premises.
  • Special alterations after disability: Max P20,000 for special alterations to the home caused by an accident that happened during the term of this policy.
  • Fire-fighting charges.
  • Removal of rubble and wreckage.

General Conditions

  • Cover property for replacement value.
  • Average condition applies or under-insurance.
  • We pay the lender first (financial interest).
  • Inflationary increases to property sums insureds.
  • Tenant's behavior.
  • Business use conditions*.

Events/Items Not Covered

  • Undamaged items (refer to the wording).
  • Loss or damage not covered caused by:
    • Any process involving the use of water.
    • Theft, attempted theft, and malicious damage.
    • Movement of the land supporting the residence.
    • An event at an unoccupied home unoccupied for 30 consecutive days.
    • Arising from or during demolition, alteration, construction, cleaning, renovation, repair or similar.
    • Faulty design or workmanship.
    • Any loss or damage caused to the Private Residence not being maintained.
    • Loss or damage caused by deliberate power cuts or load shedding.
    • Pre-existing damages to your residence.
    • Loss, damage, injury or Liability caused deliberately by You.

Indemnity under the Policy

  • Costs you are liable for
  • Legal costs of the other person that you are liable for
  • Costs you incur with our written consent to settle or defend a claim against you

Indemnity is limited to the amount of P500,000.00 at the time of the event. This amount applies to any single event, or to any series of events that result from the same incident.

Exclusions

  • Accident / incident occurred before the policy inception date
  • Injuries to certain individuals:
    • Passengers in trailers or caravans
    • Passengers outside the cab at the time of the event
  • Injuries to the insured, family members, or employees under certain conditions
  • Damage to properties
  • Compensation under motor vehicle insurance legislation
  • Any claim arising out of contractual liability
